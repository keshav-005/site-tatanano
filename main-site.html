Want to know how Nano was born?

One sunny day, Sir Ratan Tata was driving through a crowded street. He noticed a family of four precariously riding a single bike — drenched in sweat, struggling, and exposed to danger. This vision struck a chord deep in his heart. He wondered: could India have an affordable, safe car for everyone? That day marked the beginning of a dream — a dream that became a mission. And now, that mission is realized in the form of Tata Nano.
